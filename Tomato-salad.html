Marinated Tomato Salad

 

This is a refreshing salad, easy to make to accompany a casual summer dinner.

  1. Cut up 4 tomatoes
  2. Put in a bowl and drizzle olive oil over them
  3. Add a touch of white vinegar
  4. Sprinkle on dried oregano and a touch of salt
  5. Cut strips of fresh basil, and stir all
  6. Allow to chill for a few minutes
